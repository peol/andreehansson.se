about me

For you that don't know me, my name's Andrée Hansson and live in Sweden, I work at presis! as Lead Front-end Developer, I've also done a few presentations (both internally and externally) on front-end development. I'm an avid Open-Source Developer and have contributed to such projects as jQuery, basket.js, Move The Web Forward and more. I have also had the privilege to work with Addy Osmani as a technical reviewer on some of his great articles and posts (and recently, his new book Learning JavaScript Design Patterns). For more information and other fun stuff, you can check out my Twitter and GitHub pages.

This site was thrown together using jekyll, theme implemented from scratch but is heavily influenced by Minimum theme.
